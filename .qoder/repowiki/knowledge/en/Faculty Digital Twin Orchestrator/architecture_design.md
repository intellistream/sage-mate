- **Deterministic Planning & Shadow Comparison**: The `DeterministicWorkflowPlanner` generates a `PlanSpec` based on heuristic intent matching, which is then validated by `WorkflowPolicyValidator` against a `WorkflowPolicy`. An optional LLM-based shadow planner proposes alternative plans for offline comparison and evaluation.
- **Policy-Enforced Execution**: The `FacultyTwinWorkflowSupport` service executes the approved plan as a linear or DAG-based pipeline. Each step (e.g., `retrieve_knowledge`, `record_artifact_memory`) is checked against the policy for allowed side effects, latency budgets, and evidence source permissions before execution.
- **Context-Aware Routing**: `WorkflowRequestContext` aggregates session identity, consent status, and artifact availability to dynamically constrain the `EvidenceContract` and available workflow steps, ensuring privacy-compliant memory retrieval and admin-only operation isolation.
